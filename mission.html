Our mission

The inspiration

The inspiration behind JIVR comes from the challenges presented by urban congestion around the world. From the very start, we have been focused on using clever design and the best technology to provide transport options for everyone who wants to enjoy city life without the lifestyle compromises demanded by other means.

We are a team of designers, engineers and two-wheeled artists working together to bring that vision to life every day.

JIVR is leading an electric journey towards a better city life and every time we look back we see a bigger crowd behind us.
